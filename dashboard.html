VTADS Project

This research proposes to combine measurements from a high sensitivity GPS receiver with data coming from a self contained inertian navigation system and other complementary autonomous sensors such as odometers ans magnetometers. Moreover, in order to provide an affordable solution, the targeted system will be based exclusively on the use of very low cost sensors. It is expected that this project will help reduce the environmental footprint of motor vehicles in addition to having a significant positive impact on overall vehicle safety.
